## AMBIGUOUS PATTERN CATEGORIES

### Too Vague
- "help" → need clarification
- "it's broken" → what is broken?
- "doesn't work" → what doesn't work?
- "slow" → what is slow?
- "error" → what error?

### Multiple Meanings
- "install graphics" → driver? software? settings?
- "change display" → resolution? monitors? settings?
- "network problem" → wifi? ethernet? vpn?
- "sound issue" → no sound? crackling? wrong device?

### Context Needed
- "how do i fix it" → fix what?
- "where is the file" → which file?
- "which version" → of what?
- "is it compatible" → is what compatible?

### Open-Ended
- "what should i install"
- "which is better"
- "how do i start"
- "what do you recommend"
- "tell me about"

## RESPONSE STRATEGY FOR AMBIGUOUS QUESTIONS

1. **Clarification Request**: Ask for more details
2. **Common Interpretation**: Provide most likely answer
3. **LLM Fallback**: Use AI to interpret and respond
4. **Multiple Options**: "Did you mean A, B, or C?"
5. **Context Check**: Look at conversation history
